## Section 1: Short Title

This Act shall be known as the "Electric Power Industry Reform Act of 2001."

### Maharlikanism Analysis

The EPIRA was enacted to restructure the power sector, ostensibly to lower electricity rates and improve service. However, decades later, the Philippines continues to have some of the **highest electricity rates in Asia**.

Maharlikanism's proposed **Fourth Branch of Government** — the Resources Branch — would directly address this by:

1. Reclassifying essential utilities (electricity, water, internet) as **public goods** under constitutional protection
2. Establishing a Resources Council with power to regulate and nationalize failing utilities
3. Setting maximum profit margins for private utility operators

> The privatization of electricity has been one of the greatest failures of post-EDSA governance.
