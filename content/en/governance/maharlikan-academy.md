## A National Institution for Maharlikan Identity

The **Maharlikan Academy** is proposed as a constitutional institution — similar to the French Académie française — charged with preserving, studying, and promoting the cultures, languages, and traditions of Maharlika.

### Core Functions

1. **Language preservation** — Documenting and revitalizing indigenous languages
2. **Cultural research** — Funding academic study of pre-colonial Maharlikan civilization
3. **Education standards** — Developing culturally-grounded curriculum for all levels
4. **Arts patronage** — Supporting Maharlikan artists, writers, and performers

### The Cultural Crisis

Filipinos today know more about American pop culture than their own Baybayin script. They can name Hollywood celebrities but not the datus of their ancestors. This is not an accident — it is the legacy of systematic cultural erasure.

The Maharlikan Academy would reverse this by making cultural education a constitutional priority, not an afterthought.
