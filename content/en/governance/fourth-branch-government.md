## The Resources Branch

Maharlikanism proposes a **Fourth Branch of Government** dedicated to managing Maharlika's natural and public resources.

The three traditional branches (Executive, Legislative, Judicial) were designed to balance *political power*. They were never designed to manage *economic resources* in the public interest.

### Powers and Responsibilities

The Resources Branch would have authority over:

- **Natural resources**: minerals, forests, fisheries, water
- **Public utilities**: electricity, water, telecommunications, internet
- **Strategic industries**: shipping, logistics, agriculture

### Why This is Necessary

The current system allows oligarchs to acquire and exploit public resources with minimal accountability. The Resources Branch would serve as a permanent, constitutional counterweight to private interests in essential sectors.
